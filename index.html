Common Questions

What is AcademeForge?

AcademeForge is a learning platform built to help students move from confusion to clarity. We focus on strong fundamentals, practical tasks, and consistency—so learning actually turns into usable skills.

— Devraj Kumar, Founder & CEO (Bihar, India)

Who is this for?

Anyone interested in learning real-world skills such as editing, design, coding basics, or freelancing — regardless of age.

Do you teach school syllabus?

No. AcademeForge does not teach school or board syllabus. We focus on skills, mindset, and application.

Is AcademeForge active right now?

AcademeForge is currently in a rebuild phase, focusing on mastering skills and refining its learning model before a full relaunch.
